About Actollo

The company name, 'Actollo' is from the necessity of 'Activities' in a fitness regiment, and 'Apollo', the Greek God of the sun and light known for his bravery.
Like the name implies, Actollo aims to provide its users with the assistance to jumpdrive their fitness exercise with the attitude of Apollo.

What Actollo Can Do

Actollo can provide users with a daily update on their personal fitness regiments. With its smart weather-adaptation feature, Actollo can change the fitness regiment depending on the weather conditions of the day. All of the athletic programs are designed personally for each athlete, allowing each to achieve their unique goals with the most effective program.
